SCENARIO: Post-operative Surgical Wound Care
SCENARIO_ID: scenario_test_001

HISTORY TAKING GUIDELINES
- Introduce yourself clearly to the patient
- Verify patient identity using name and date of birth
- Explain the wound care procedure before starting
- Obtain verbal consent
- Maintain empathetic and professional communication

WOUND ASSESSMENT GUIDELINES
- Observe wound for redness, swelling, discharge, or odor
- A clean surgical wound should have intact sutures
- Mild pain is expected; fever is not
- Document findings accurately

WOUND CLEANING GUIDELINES
- Perform hand hygiene before touching the wound
- Use aseptic technique
- Clean from the center outward
- Avoid contaminating sterile equipment

DRESSING GUIDELINES
- Select an appropriate sterile dressing
- Maintain sterility throughout application
- Secure dressing without excessive pressure
- Inform patient after completing procedure
